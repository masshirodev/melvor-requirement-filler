Never grind for a cost again. Wherever Melvor shows you something you can't afford, this mod puts a button right next to it that hands you exactly what's missing.

Cost buttons

  • Shop — fill a purchase's item and currency costs.
  • Agility — fill an obstacle's or pillar's build costs.
  • Astrology — add the Stardust for the next upgrade, or Add max for every remaining level of a modifier.
  • Smithing, Fletching, Crafting, Runecrafting, Herblore, Summoning — a quantity dropdown that adds the materials to make the selected recipe N times.
  • Cooking — the same, for every cooking category.
  • Firemaking — logs to burn N times, plus the oil for "Oil my Logs".
  • Farming — seeds to plant N times, and the items to unlock a plot.
  • Cartography — paper, dig site map creation, map upgrades and refinement slots.
  • Township — town resources to build or upgrade N times (with a Max option that funds every build remaining until the next upgrade), plus repairs.
  • Township tasks — a Claim button that completes the task and pays out the rewards. Works on goals you can't buy your way out of, like "Defeat 50 Skeletons".

Costs are read from the game's own modifier-aware getters, so cost-reduction bonuses are respected — you get the reduced amount actually shown on the card, never the base cost.

Every dropdown has x1 / x10 / x100 / x1000 presets and a custom amount field.

Item Adder

A settings page with a searchable, category-filtered grid of every item and currency in the game. Select as many as you like, set a quantity, and add them straight to your bank. Large batches are added in throttled chunks, so the game doesn't freeze.

Usage

Open the Mod Manager, go to this mod's Settings, and click Open Item Adder for the grid. The cost buttons appear on their screens automatically.

This is a cheat mod — it's meant for sandboxing, testing and messing about, not for legit-mode play.
